Biriyani:

Description:

Put the boiling water in a saucepan, add the washed rice, boil for 5 minutes and drain.
Layer the chicken mixture with the rice starting with the rice then the chicken mixture and
repeat this twice. Garnish with cumin seeds, and ginger. Lower the heat and simmer for a
further 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 300g basmati rice.
  • 25g butter.
  • 1 bay leaf.
  • 3 cardamom pods.
  • 4 skinless chicken breasts, cut into large chunks.
  • 1 tsp turmeric.
  • small cinnamon stick.
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced.
  • 85g raisins
  • 850ml chicken stock
